# Platform Templates

Use these as lightweight output targets for the `research-survey` skill.

## X or Twitter

Default structure:

- one sharp claim
- two to four supporting observations
- one closing implication or question

## LinkedIn

Default structure:

- broader framing
- why the topic matters now
- three concise takeaways
- optional implication for labs, industry, or students

## Zhihu or WeChat

Default structure:

- context for the topic
- route or method breakdown
- your judgment
- one short concluding takeaway section

## Newsletter or long note

Default structure:

- thesis
- research map
- debates and limitations
- your take
- next papers or questions worth following
