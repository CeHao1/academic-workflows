# Robot Learning Angles

Useful recurring angles for embodied AI and robot learning content:

- benchmark generalization versus open-world robustness
- model scale versus data coverage
- what is genuinely learned versus what is system engineering
- sim-to-real transfer limits
- evaluation blind spots
- teleoperation, labeling, and interface costs
- dexterous manipulation bottlenecks
- world models in real-world control
- bimanual coordination and long-horizon tasks
- what current robot foundation models still fail to capture
